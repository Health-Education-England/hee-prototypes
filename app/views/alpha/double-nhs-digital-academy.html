What is the NHS Digital Academy?

The NHS Digital Academy is a virtual organisation set up to develop a new generation of excellent digital leaders who can drive the information and technology transformation of the NHS.

The Academy offers a range of learning programmes, including:

  • Digital Leadership Programme: This is a year-long programme that equips leaders with the skills and knowledge they need to lead digital transformation in the NHS.
  • Digital Acceleration Programme: This is a six-month programme that helps individuals develop the skills and knowledge they need to accelerate digital transformation in their organisations.
  • Digital Skills Programme: This is a short course that provides individuals with the essential digital skills they need to work in the NHS.

The Academy also offers a range of support services, including:

  • Mentoring: The Academy provides mentors to support individuals as they develop their digital skills and knowledge.
  • Networking: The Academy provides opportunities for individuals to network with other digital leaders in the NHS.
  • Research: The Academy conducts research into digital transformation in the NHS.

The NHS Digital Academy is committed to developing the next generation of digital leaders who can drive the information and technology transformation of the NHS.
